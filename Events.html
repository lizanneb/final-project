Events

My Uncle hosted a dinner party to honor a small group of young Cuban musicians who were selected to play in New Orleans in a prestigeous band for youth who excelled in music. Also, we visited the Lizt Alonso dance company and saw some amazing Cuban dancing in a special in-studio performance. We visited a few different jazz clubs, which are popular in Cuban night life. Every night, the Cuban citizens reinact the lighting of the canon to signify the revolution. We went to the ceremony one night to see it and there were beautiful views of Havana. They also sold artwork, jewelry, and other souvineirs.
