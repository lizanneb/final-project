House

These pictures are from my Uncle's house in Havana. This estate was built specifically for embassadors to stay in while they were visiting Cuba. The house had eight main bedrooms, although I didn't get pictures of all of them. Each bedroom had it's own gigantic bathroom which was the most overwhelming part of the house for me. The kitchen was run by professional gourmet staff and there were two dining rooms. There was a small one at which we ate our family meals, and then there was a larger one for more grand events with lots of people such as the dinner party my Uncle hosted while we were visiting. There is a tennis court and pool by the house and there is a garden in the back where the chef grows his own ingredients.
